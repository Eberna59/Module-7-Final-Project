Rides

WonderPark offers thrilling rides for all ages. Below you can find details about our top attractions, along with height and safety requirements.

Shadow of Hell

Height Requirement: 54 inches | Extreme Thrill Ride.

Grey New WRLD

Height Requirement: 59 inches | Intense Psychological Experience.

Rules & Safety

To ensure the safety of all guests, please review the requirements below:

Ride Min Height Notes
Shadow of Hell 54 inches Not recommended for pregnant guests or those with heart conditions.
Grey New WRLD 59 inches Guests must sign a waiver before riding.

General Safety Reminders

  • Keep arms and legs inside the ride at all times.
  • Follow staff instructions for boarding and exiting.
  • Secure all loose items before riding.
  • Report any issues to staff immediately.
